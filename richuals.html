Ritual for Banishing Negative Thoughts and Attracting Wealth, Stability, and Opportunities

Preparation

Time

Perform this ritual during the new moon for new beginnings, or the full moon for power and abundance.

Tools

  • A white candle (for clarity and peace)
  • A green candle (for wealth and growth)
  • A black candle (for protection and banishing negativity)
  • A clear quartz crystal (for mental clarity)
  • A piece of citrine (for wealth and success)
  • A small bowl of water (for emotional stability)
  • A small bowl of salt (for grounding)
  • A vision board or notebook for writing intentions
  • Essential oils like lavender (for peace) and peppermint (for clarity)

Ritual Steps

  1. Cleansing the Space: Begin by cleansing your space with sage, palo santo, or incense. As the smoke rises, envision the negative energy dissipating from your environment, creating a clean, sacred space.
  2. Grounding and Centering: Sit in a comfortable position. Place the bowls of water and salt in front of you. Close your eyes, and take a deep breath. As you inhale, visualize pure white light filling your body, and as you exhale, release any lingering tension or negativity. Say aloud:
    "I release all that does not serve me. I banish fear, doubt, and negative thoughts. I am grounded and centered."
  3. Light the Black Candle (Banishing Negative Thoughts): As you light the black candle, focus on releasing any negative thoughts, fears, or self-doubt that hinder your progress. Visualize these thoughts being absorbed by the flame. Say:
    "With this flame, I banish all negativity from my mind and spirit. I release all thoughts that block my path. Peace fills my being."
  4. Light the White Candle (Clarity and Mental Stability): After lighting the white candle, visualize a bright light enveloping you, clearing away mental fog and inviting clarity and calm. Say:
    "I call upon peace and clarity of mind. I am calm, patient, and stable in my thoughts and actions. I am a stable and loving parent, guiding my children with wisdom."
  5. Light the Green Candle (Wealth, Growth, and Opportunities): Light the green candle and hold the citrine in your hand. Visualize a golden light filling you, symbolizing abundance, wealth, and new opportunities. Say:
    "I attract abundance in all forms. Wealth flows to me effortlessly, and I manage it with wisdom and responsibility. New opportunities in the arts, entertainment, and beyond are drawn to me like a magnet. I welcome a new home filled with love and prosperity."
  6. Infuse Your Crystals: Hold the clear quartz and citrine in your hands. Close your eyes and imagine these crystals amplifying your intentions. See them glowing brightly, charged with the energy of your desires: clarity, wealth, opportunity, stability. Say:
    "I charge these stones with my intentions. They carry my will and amplify my desires. I am aligned with success, wealth, and opportunity."
  7. Write Down Your Intentions: Take your vision board or notebook and write down your goals in the present tense. Write as if they have already happened. For example:
    • "I am free from negative thoughts."
    • "I am financially abundant, and wealth flows easily to me."
    • "I am a patient and stable parent, nurturing my children with love."
    • "I attract exciting opportunities in the entertainment world."
    • "I have a beautiful new home, filled with peace and prosperity."
    Visualize each intention as you write, feeling the emotions of already achieving these goals.
  8. Final Affirmation: Stand up and say aloud with conviction:
    "I am open to receiving the wealth, opportunities, and peace that the universe offers. I am aligned with abundance, mental clarity, and stability in all areas of my life. My new home, success, and personal growth are manifesting now."
  9. Close the Ritual: Blow out the candles in the reverse order: green, white, black. Thank the energies and elements you called upon. Let the smoke or scent from your candles fill the room with the vibration of your new reality.

    Optional: Keep the citrine with you or in a place of work to continue attracting wealth and success. Place the clear quartz in your home for clarity and stability.

After the Ritual

  • Meditate or journal for a few minutes to reflect on any insights or feelings that came up.
  • Take consistent, small actions in alignment with your intentions (e.g., apply for new opportunities, budget wisely, or practice patience with your children).
  • Revisit your vision board or notebook regularly to reinforce your desires.

This ritual combines the power of intention, energy work, and visualization to manifest the changes and opportunities you seek, while grounding you in mental stability and clarity.
